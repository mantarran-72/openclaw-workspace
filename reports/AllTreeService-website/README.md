# AllTreeService-website

This version is the higher-quality sales direction.

Open `index.html` in a browser. Keep the `assets/` folder beside it.

## Design direction

- Premium editorial local service page.
- Real work photos only.
- Large edge-to-edge imagery.
- Minimal copy.
- Typography-led layout.
- No repetitive card grids.
- No fake icons, awards, counters, or stock imagery.
- No blue gradients, blobs, glassmorphism, neon, or generic AI landing-page styling.
- Call and text-photo actions stay visible and direct.
- Final CTA uses a Yelp-style quick estimate flow: job type first, then urgency/details, then contact info.
- The demo "Send by text" button pre-fills an SMS estimate request from the form answers.

## Sales angle

Do not pitch this as "a website." Pitch it as a better sales tool:

> I rebuilt the page around how tree-service customers actually decide on mobile: they want to see real work, know you are licensed, call fast, or text photos before you drive out.

Useful points:

- Real All Tree Service job photos carry the design.
- Phone number and text-photo option are prominent.
- License number is visible.
- Local Santa Rosa / Sonoma County language is built in.
- Avoids the risky "Certified Arborist" claim unless they can document it.
