1. Living Space Wall Insulation: New & Existing Stud bays exposed during
construction where the existing insulation has been removed that surround the
conditioned living space shall be insulated w/ min R-15 insulation @ [E] 2x4 stud
bays & R-21 @ [N] & [E] 2x6 stud bays.  See the T-24 report & spec. div. 07050
#3 for additional requirements.
2. Attic Insulation: Provide min R-38 fiberglass insulation in the new attic space at
the ceiling level between the ceiling joists or truss bottom chords at the addition
and over the existing conditioned living space where the existing insulation has
been removed.  See the T-24 report & spec. div. 07050 #5 for additional
requirements. Provide a 1” baffle opening at the vented eve blocks and where a
radiant barrier is installed to provide venting for the attic space. Provide &
present alt pricing to the owners to upgrade the existing attic space to also have
R-49 insulation typ.
3. Under Floor Crawl Space insulation: Provide min R-19 fiberglass insulation in
the new crawl space floor joist bays under the new construction typical. Provide
& present alt pricing to the owners to provide insulation in the existing crawl
space between the existing girders. See the T-24 report & spec. div. 07050 #
4. [E] Roof Ply To Remain W/ A [N] Radiant Barrier To Be Installed Over The
[N[ Added Living Space: The existing Ply Sheathing shall remain as-is. Staple or
adhere a new radiant barrier to the under side of the roof ply sheathing & rafters
as MFG by Eagle shield or equal. Provide a min 1” air space between the radiant
barrier & the insulation typical.
5. Roofing: [E] Asphalt comp roofing to remain as-is.
6. Clear 1” Air Space: Provide a 1” clear air space between the radiant barrier &
the insulation typical.
7. Gyp Board: Fully line the interior living space walls w/ min 1/2” gyp bd. At the
ceiling provide Min 5/8” gyp board or 1/2” ceiling density GYP board where the
framing members are spaced at 24” o.c. See spec division 09100 for additional
GYP board requirements typical.
8. New Siding: Provide new siding per the exterior elevations.
9. Items Shown In The Background: Items shown in the background are rough
sketch lines only to clarify the location & orientation of the section only. Trim
details are not shown, or if they are shown they are not accurately represented.
Consult with the owners for all trim details & finish items typical.

foundation @ min 5% for the first 10’ typical. See detail [1/C1] & the grading
plan for additional requirements typical.
11. Eve Blocking: The existing eve blocking is to remain as is. Protect during
construction and do not reduce any of the existing attic ventilation typical.
12. Slope Concrete Away From the building: Provide expansion joint material
between the exterior slabs & the building. Slope all the exterior concrete slabs
away from the building @ ¼’ per ft typical.
