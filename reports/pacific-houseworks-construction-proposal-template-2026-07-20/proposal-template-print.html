Included Scope Of Work

1. Project Setup And Protection

  • Mobilization, layout, site setup, and normal daily cleanup.
  • Protection of adjacent finished areas, walkways, driveways, landscaping, and remaining occupied portions of the home.
  • Dust control and temporary separation of the work area where practical.
  • Coordination with client, inspectors, suppliers, and subcontractors.

2. Demolition And Preparation

  • Selective demolition required for the included scope.
  • Careful removal of affected finishes, framing, fixtures, and materials only where required.
  • Loading, haul-off, and disposal of construction debris generated by Pacific Houseworks work.
  • Protection of existing systems and finishes intended to remain.

3. Framing, Structural, And Building Shell

  • Framing, blocking, sheathing, hardware, and structural tie-ins required by the plans and included scope.
  • Foundation, raised floor, wall, roof, siding, or exterior envelope work described in the drawings and proposal assumptions.
  • Weather-resistive barrier, flashing, sealants, and exterior trim at new or disturbed openings.
  • Installation of owner-furnished or contractor-supplied doors/windows as listed in the allowance schedule.

4. Rough Trades

  • Electrical work required for the included scope, subject to panel capacity and field verification.
  • Plumbing work required for the included scope, if any.
  • HVAC modifications required for the included scope, if any.
  • Low-voltage, appliance, fixture, and equipment coordination where specifically listed.
